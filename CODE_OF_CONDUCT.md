# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in OmniAgent Island
a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or
invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression,
level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal
appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

1. Being respectful in disagreements
2. Accepting constructive feedback
3. Focusing on what is best for the community
4. Showing empathy toward other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

1. Harassment or discriminatory language
2. Trolling, personal attacks, or sustained disruption
3. Publishing private information without permission
4. Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of
acceptable behavior and may take appropriate and fair corrective action in response
to any behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and in public spaces when an
individual is officially representing the project.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to the maintainers through the contact path described in
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and
fairly.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
